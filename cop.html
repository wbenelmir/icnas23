CALL FOR PAPERS - ICNAS'23

International Conference on Networking and Advanced Systems ICNAS'23

The Sixth conference ICNAS 2023 is a scientific event that brings together scientific expertise on several areas of scientific research. This biennial conference is the successor of the conference ICNAS'13, ICNAS'15, ICNAS'17, ICNAS'19 and ICNAS'21. In fact, the areas of interest ICNAS 2023 are valid with a cross between different research themes. Through this conference, the scientific frontiers are pushed and multidisciplinary investigations allow researchers and young researchers to propose their ideas and contributions. The conference deals with topics relating to networking, management, and systems that revolve around this technology. The systems are advanced systems by their design, implementation, and applications

We invite you to submit your original technical papers to this event. Accepted and presented papers will be published in the ICNAS'2023 Conference Proceedings. The extended version of selected high-quality papers will be considered for publication in the international journals.

ICNAS 2023 is organized by the Networks and Systems Laboratory - LRS. It will take place at the Center for Development of Advanced Technologies Algiers, Algeria

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES:

All submissions must be original, unpublished, and not considered elsewhere for publication. The initial submission for review will be limited to 6 pages, including references, figures and tables (at least 10pt font, 2-column format). The papers accepted and presented in ICNAS 2023 will be published in the conference proceedings.

IEEE LaTeX and Microsoft Word templates, as well as formatting instructions, are available online on this website.

Contributions should be submitted electronically as PDF, using the IEEE conference publishing template, via the conference submission website (or just click below).
https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=6thicnas23

  • Non Student: 25000 DA
